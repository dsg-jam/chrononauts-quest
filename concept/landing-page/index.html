Chrononauts: Fighting the Past

A thrilling escape room adventure through time

Deep within a bustling metropolis, Thiemo and Lara, two brilliant detectives with the extraordinary ability to travel through time, receive a cryptic message from the past. A renowned scientist has vanished without a trace, leaving behind a trail of perplexing clues and a groundbreaking invention - a machine that could solve the world's energy crisis.

With the fate of the future hanging in the balance, Thiemo and Lara embark on a daring mission to the year 1920. Their task: to unravel the mystery of the missing scientist, decipher the secrets of the stolen machine, and restore order to a world teetering on the brink of chaos.

Escape the Past, Save the Future

Step into Chrononauts: Fighting the Past and be transported to a world of intrigue, suspense, and mind-bending puzzles. Your mission: decipher the cryptic clues left behind by the missing scientist and navigate the intricate mechanisms of his revolutionary machine.

Encounter a cast of enigmatic characters, each with their own secrets and motives. Use your intellect, sharp observation skills, and teamwork to overcome obstacles, decipher hidden codes, and uncover the truth behind the scientist's disappearance.

The Key to Time Travel Awaits

To unlock the portal to 1920, you'll need to find the hidden key that holds the power to transport you through time. This key is said to be concealed within the depths of the game's intricate puzzles and challenges. Can you decipher the clues and locate the key before time runs out?
