# Concept

Name: `Chrononauts: Fighting the Past`

## Story Brief

Thiemo and Lara are two brilliant detectives who can travel through time.
One day, they receive a mysterious call for help from the year 1920. A famous scientist has disappeared without a trace, and his groundbreaking invention, a machine that could solve the energy crisis, has been stolen.
Thiemo and Lara must travel to 1920, find the scientist, solve the mystery of the stolen machine, and save the present. To travel to 1920, they still need a key, can you find it?
